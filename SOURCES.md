# Sources and Acknowledgments

This curriculum draws on foundational texts, methodological papers, and practical guides in empirical macroeconometrics.

## Core Textbooks

### Econometrics Foundations
- **Wooldridge, J.M.** (2010). *Econometric Analysis of Cross Section and Panel Data*, 2nd ed. MIT Press.
  - Chapters 10-14 on panel data methods
- **Angrist, J.D. & Pischke, J-S.** (2009). *Mostly Harmless Econometrics*. Princeton University Press.
  - Causal inference framework, IV, DID

### Time Series and VAR Methods
- **Kilian, L. & Lütkepohl, H.** (2017). *Structural Vector Autoregressive Analysis*. Cambridge University Press.
  - Comprehensive treatment of SVAR identification
- **Hamilton, J.D.** (1994). *Time Series Analysis*. Princeton University Press.
  - Foundational time series methods

### Bayesian Econometrics
- **Gelman, A. et al.** (2013). *Bayesian Data Analysis*, 3rd ed. CRC Press.
  - Chapters on hierarchical models, MCMC diagnostics
- **Koop, G.** (2003). *Bayesian Econometrics*. Wiley.
  - Applied Bayesian methods for economists

### DSGE Modeling
- **Galí, J.** (2015). *Monetary Policy, Inflation, and the Business Cycle*, 2nd ed. Princeton University Press.
  - New Keynesian DSGE foundations

## Methodological Papers

### Local Projections
- Jordà, Ò. (2005). "Estimation and Inference of Impulse Responses by Local Projections." *AER*.
- Ramey, V.A. (2016). "Macroeconomic Shocks and Their Propagation." *Handbook of Macroeconomics*.

### Modern Difference-in-Differences
- Goodman-Bacon, A. (2021). "Difference-in-Differences with Variation in Treatment Timing." *JoE*.
- Sun, L. & Abraham, S. (2021). "Estimating Dynamic Treatment Effects in Event Studies." *JoE*.
- Callaway, B. & Sant'Anna, P.H.C. (2021). "Difference-in-Differences with Multiple Time Periods." *JoE*.
- Roth, J. et al. (2023). "What's Trending in Difference-in-Differences?" *JoE*.

### Synthetic Control
- Abadie, A., Diamond, A., & Hainmueller, J. (2010). "Synthetic Control Methods." *JASA*.
- Abadie, A. (2021). "Using Synthetic Controls." *JEL*.

### Bayesian VARs
- Litterman, R.B. (1986). "Forecasting with Bayesian Vector Autoregressions." *JBES*.
- Sims, C.A. & Zha, T. (1998). "Bayesian Methods for Dynamic Multivariate Models." *IER*.
- Uhlig, H. (2005). "What are the Effects of Monetary Policy on Output?" *JME*.

### Causal Machine Learning
- Athey, S. & Imbens, G.W. (2019). "Machine Learning Methods That Economists Should Know About." *ARE*.
- Chernozhukov, V. et al. (2018). "Double/Debiased Machine Learning." *Econometrics Journal*.
- Wager, S. & Athey, S. (2018). "Estimation of Heterogeneous Treatment Effects using Random Forests." *JASA*.

### Global Financial Cycle
- Miranda-Agrippino, S. & Rey, H. (2020). "The Global Financial Cycle After Lehman." *AEA P&P*.
- Rey, H. (2015). "Dilemma not Trilemma." *NBER WP*.

## Software and Implementation Guides

### R Packages
- `fixest` - Fast fixed-effects estimation (Bergé, 2018)
- `did` - Callaway-Sant'Anna estimator
- `synthdid` - Synthetic DID
- `vars` - VAR estimation

### Python Libraries
- `statsmodels` - Econometric modeling
- `PyMC` / `NumPyro` - Bayesian inference
- `EconML` - Causal ML (Microsoft)

### MATLAB/Dynare
- Dynare documentation (dynare.org)
- IRIS Toolbox for macroeconomic modeling

## Practical Guides

- Cameron, A.C. & Miller, D.L. (2015). "A Practitioner's Guide to Cluster-Robust Inference." *JHR*.
- Fernández-Villaverde, J. et al. (2007). "ABCs (and Ds) of Understanding VARs." *AER*.
